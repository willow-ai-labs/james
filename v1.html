Risk Architecture

Capital Preservation

Risk management is not a feature—it is the foundation. Every decision prioritizes preservation over aggressive returns.

01

Position Sizing Limits

No single position exceeds predefined percentage of total capital. Prevents concentration risk and catastrophic loss.

02

Drawdown Caps

Portfolio losses at maximum threshold trigger automatic suspension. System requires manual review before resuming.

03

Market Selection

Pilot focuses on highly liquid markets with tight spreads and deep order books. Minimizes slippage and execution risk.

Success Criteria

Measurable Outcomes

The pilot is evaluated on objective metrics, not subjective assessments.

CRITERION 01

System Uptime & Reliability

Trading engine must operate without unplanned downtime or execution failures. Repeated failures disqualify the pilot.

CRITERION 02

Risk Limit Adherence

All trades must comply with predefined position sizing and drawdown caps. A single violation constitutes failure, regardless of profitability.

CRITERION 03

Performance Target

Target return of approximately 10% over evaluation period. This is an objective, not a guarantee. Below-threshold performance does not automatically disqualify if other criteria are met.

CRITERION 04

Operational Transparency

Dashboard must provide accurate, real-time data throughout pilot. Any discrepancies are grounds for immediate termination.
