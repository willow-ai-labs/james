Methodology

Rule-Based Execution

The JadeFi platform operates on a foundation of systematic, rule-based execution. Every trade decision follows predefined parameters—eliminating discretionary intervention and the emotional biases that historically undermine trading performance. The system focuses exclusively on one to two liquid markets, maintaining depth of expertise rather than breadth of exposure.

Automated trading removes the human elements that traditionally introduce inconsistency: fear, greed, fatigue, and overconfidence. By codifying every decision into executable rules, the platform ensures identical responses to identical market conditions. This systematic approach enables continuous operation without the degradation in judgment that affects human traders during extended sessions.

Position sizing follows strict mathematical models. Drawdown caps are enforced automatically, without exception. The system cannot be convinced, cajoled, or pressured into exceeding its risk parameters. This architectural discipline distinguishes JadeFi from discretionary approaches where "just this once" exceptions lead to catastrophic outcomes.

Position transparency

Hard Risk
Limits

Non-negotiable constraints enforced at the system architecture level. These limits cannot be overridden regardless of market conditions or perceived opportunity.

  • 01

    Position Sizing

    Maximum position size capped as percentage of total capital. No single trade can exceed predefined exposure limits.

  • 02

    Drawdown Caps

    Automatic position reduction or halt when cumulative losses reach threshold. Preservation of capital supersedes profit pursuit.

  • 03

    Market Concentration

    Operations limited to one or two highly liquid markets. No expansion into additional instruments without explicit approval.

  • 04

    Execution Rules

    All trades follow codified rules. Manual intervention disabled by design. System operates identically regardless of operator access.
